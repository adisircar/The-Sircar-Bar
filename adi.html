Who is Adi?

Adi Sircar

Adi is an engineer, entrepreneur, and author with a passion for innovation.

He currently works at Dow Jones, publisher of The Wall Street Journal, delivering trusted news to business leaders since 1882.

Adi is a proud patriot, family man, and inquisitive learner. He founded Ocubloc to help cataract surgery patients, built a smart helmet for NASA to make spacewalks safe, and served thousands of lovely guests at his restaurants.

Adi loves playing soccer, learning new cultures, and skateboarding with Archie.
